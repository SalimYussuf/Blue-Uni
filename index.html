Entry Requirements

Find out what you need to join Socotra University programs.

Undergraduate Programs

  • ✓ KCSE candidates: Mean grade of C+ (Plus) or its equivalent.
  • ✓ IGCSE/A-Level: Minimum of two (2) principal passes and a subsidiary pass.
  • ✓ International Baccalaureate (IB): Minimum of 24 points.
  • ✓ Diploma holders: A recognized diploma with a minimum of a credit pass.

Postgraduate Programs

  • ✓ Master's: A Bachelor’s degree with at least Upper Second Class Honors.
  • ✓ PhD: A recognized Master’s degree in a relevant field of study.
  • ✓ Work Experience: Some professional programs (e.g., MBA) require 2-3 years of proven work experience.
  • ✓ Approved Research Proposal (for PhD applicants).

Scholarships & Aid

We believe financial constraints should never be a barrier to world-class education.

MERIT-BASED

Vice-Chancellor's Excellence Award

Awarded to the top 5% of direct entrants based on national exam performance. Covers 100% of tuition and accommodation fees.

Deadline: July 15, 2025
NEED-BASED

Socotra Opportunity Grant

Designed for students from underserved communities. Provides partial to full tuition coverage depending on assessed financial need.

Deadline: August 1, 2025
SPORTS / TALENT

Elite Athlete Scholarship

For exceptionally talented student-athletes representing the university at national or international tournaments. Includes housing and stipends.

Deadline: Rolling Admission
